DVM-Car Dataset: A Large-Scale Dataset for Automotive Applications

Welcome to this webpage! We are building a large-scale automobile dataset that contains images of numerous car models and the corresponding car specification and sales figures over more than 10 years period, with the hope to better support and serve the needs of researchers in various fields. Our dataset primarily constitutes two sections: the image section and the data table section.

Car Images

The image section contains 1.4 million car images. The images have been organised under the categorisation “brand–model–year–colour”.

As the above figures illustrate:

  • Our car images are divided into eight categories regarding their observation angles.
  • In the dataset, the background information of the images is stored in the corresponding 'pkl' files (background removal is required in many studies as researchers do not want the bias caused by the background information).
  • Due to the requirement of data privacy, the car number plates in images are obscured.

    Data Tables

    • Basic table contains the car attributes such as model name, model ID and brand name.
    • Sales table contains over ten years car sales data in UK/GB.
    • Price table contains the entry-level (i.e. the cheapest trim price) new car prices accross years.
    • Trim table contains trim attributes like the selling price (trim level), engine type and engine size.
    • Ad table includes the information of more than 0.25 million used car ads.
    • Image table contains car images attributes like colour, viewpoint.
